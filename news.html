Digital Transformation in Payroll & HR: Key Takeaways from the CorePay Masterclass

The CorePay Payroll Masterclass 2024, held at the breathtaking Victoria Falls Safari Lodge, brought together industry experts and professionals to discuss the future of payroll and human capital management in the digital age. With the theme "Digital Transformation in Payroll and Human Capital Management: Navigating the New Frontier," the event explored how technology is reshaping HR operations, payroll compliance, and workforce engagement.

The Impact of Digitalization on Payroll & HR
  • Automation of Payroll Processing
  • AI & Data-Driven Decision Making
  • Employee Self-Service Portals
  • Cloud-Based HR Solutions
  • Compliance & Risk Management

Looking Ahead: The Future of Payroll & HR

The CorePay Payroll Masterclass 2024 provided a clear roadmap for companies looking to stay ahead in the digital era. As technology continues to evolve, organizations must invest in modern HR systems, prioritize employee engagement, and leverage data analytics for strategic decision-making. At CorePay, we remain committed to leading the way in payroll and HR digital transformation. Stay connected with us for more insights and upcoming events that will shape the future of human capital management.

CorePay’s Industry Recognitions & Awards: A Testament to Excellence

At CorePay, we are proud to celebrate a year of outstanding achievements and industry recognition. Our dedication to innovation, exceptional service, and leadership in human capital management has been acknowledged through multiple prestigious awards. These recognitions highlight our commitment to excellence and continuous growth in the industry.

Celebrating Leadership Excellence

One of the most significant moments of the year was our CEO, Tandi Mapolisa, being honored as the Platinum Winner at the Zimbabwe CEO’s Event held at Cresta Hotel on November 23, 2024. This award is a testament to Tandi’s visionary leadership, her ability to inspire teams, and her strategic foresight in transforming CorePay into a leader in payroll and human capital management solutions.

Recognition at the IPMZ Awards

On November 29, 2024, CorePay received two major awards at the Institute of People Management of Zimbabwe (IPMZ) Awards in Harare:

  • First Runner-Up for Recruitment Agency of the Year
  • Best Human Capital Information System

These accolades reflect our impact in the HR industry and our continued efforts to provide world-class human capital management solutions. We are honored to be recognized for our contribution to shaping the future of HR in Zimbabwe.

SME Awards: A Fast-Growing Company

At the SME Awards on December 5, 2024, CorePay was recognized as the Fast-Growing Company of the Year. This achievement underscores our relentless dedication to innovation, client satisfaction, and expanding our solutions to meet the evolving needs of businesses across the region.

CorePay at the ZAFTA Awards: Supporting Creative Excellence

Beyond the HR and business sectors, CorePay was proud to be part of the ZAFTA Awards on November 30, 2024. This event celebrated excellence in Zimbabwe’s television industry, and CorePay had the honor of presenting an award, reinforcing our support for local talent and the creative economy.

Looking Ahead: A Future of Innovation and Growth

These industry recognitions motivate us to continue pushing boundaries and delivering cutting-edge payroll and HR solutions. Our journey is fueled by a commitment to excellence, and we look forward to achieving even greater milestones in the years ahead. We extend our heartfelt gratitude to our clients, partners, and team members who have contributed to these successes.
