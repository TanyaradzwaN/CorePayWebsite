Payroll Outsourcing

Avoid payroll headaches and focus on growing your business by outsourcing your salary administration to CorePay. Our team of payroll experts is well-versed in both employer needs and Zimbabwe's payroll statutory requirements. We help businesses of all sizes improve productivity by handling non-core activities, allowing your team to concentrate on what matters most.

Payroll Software

For organizations that prefer to manage payroll in-house, we offer a tailor-made payroll software solution designed to seamlessly integrate with your environment. Our CorePay Payroll Software is available on a licensing basis, giving you the flexibility to choose quarterly, bi-annual, or annual payment options.

HR Solution

Save time and improve payroll accuracy with the latest in time and attendance software. However you prefer to track employee time, we have flexible options to fit your business.

Hire In Africa

At CorePay, we understand that finding the right talent can be a time-consuming and resource-draining process. That's why our Recruitment Outsourcing Services are designed to take the burden off your shoulders. Employer Of Record

Expand your workforce globally without the hassle. CorePay’s EOR solution lets you hire employees in any location while we handle all the legal and administrative responsibilities, payroll, taxes, benefits, and compliance. Focus on growing your business, and let us take care of the rest.
