Hire in Africa

Talent Outsourcing for Global Companies

Looking to hire top-tier talent from Africa to support your global operations? CorePay offers a comprehensive outsourcing solution that helps businesses based outside of Zimbabwe and the African continent access highly skilled employees from across Africa. Whether you’re a small business or a large enterprise, CorePay connects you with the best talent, while managing all HR, compliance, payroll, and legal requirements.


Africa is home to a rapidly growing pool of talented professionals with expertise across various industries including technology, finance, healthcare, customer service, and more. Hiring from Africa can give your company access to cost-effective, highly skilled, and motivated employees, all while enhancing diversity and expanding your global reach.

Hire In Africa

How Does It Work?

  • You Provide the Role Specifications: Share the job description and requirements, and CorePay will help you find the ideal candidate from Africa.
  • CorePay Manages HR: We handle the hiring process, legalities, payroll, taxes, and compliance.
  • Employee Reports to You: Your employee works directly with your team, while CorePay supports them throughout their employment.

Why Choose CorePay’s Outsourcing Solution?

  • Global Access to Talent: With CorePay’s network, you gain access to skilled professionals from various sectors across Africa.
  • Compliant and Risk-Free: We ensure that all employment practices follow local laws and international standards, minimizing any risks.
  • Scalability: Whether hiring one employee or scaling up an entire team, we can accommodate businesses of all sizes and needs.
