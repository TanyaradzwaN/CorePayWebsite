CorepayHR's Leave Management system is an integrated tool that streamlines the process of managing employee leaves across an organization. It ensures operational efficiency and provides seamless support to both employees and HR teams. Key features of this module include. Key features include:

  • Self-service for employees to request and track leave.
  • Automated leave approval workflow.
  • Integration with payroll to reflect leave deductions or adjustments.
  • Leave balance tracking and reporting.

CorepayHR's Training and Development module is designed to enhance employee skills and improve organizational performance by providing a centralized platform for tracking and managing training and development activities. This module supports both individual employee growth and organizational development. Key Features:

  • Training Record Keeping: Tracks training history, certifications, and outcomes.
  • Growth: Identifies skill gaps and aligns training to enhance employee and organizational development.
  • Personalized Learning Plans: Tailors training to individual roles and career paths.
  • Reporting & Analytics: Evaluates training effectiveness to guide HR decisions.
  • Payroll integration
  • Compliance Training: Ensures employees complete required regulatory and safety training.

The Business Intelligence (BI) module helps HR teams leverage data analytics to make informed decisions that positively impact the bottom line. By providing valuable insights into workforce performance, employee engagement, and operational trends, HR departments can take proactive steps to drive business growth.

  • Data-Driven Insights: Generates reports and metrics on performance, attendance, compensation, and retention trends.
  • Continuous Feedback: Provides regular feedback to foster improvement.
  • Performance Reviews: Periodic evaluations to assess strengths, areas for development, and achievements.
  • Self & Peer Reviews: Allows employees to self-assess and engage in peer reviews.
  • Development Plans: Creates personalized plans for skill enhancement based on reviews.
  • Progress Tracking: Tracks employee progress in real-time for timely support.
