CorePay In-House
Payroll Solution & Training

For organizations that prefer to manage their salaries in-house, CorePay offers a tailor-made payroll software solution designed to suit your specific business needs. The CorePay Payroll Software is available on a licensing basis, with flexible payment options. To ensure smooth operations, CorePay also offers specialized payroll training to equip your salary managers and administrators with the knowledge to handle payroll processing effectively.

Service Benefits
  • Cost-Effective
  • Customizable
  • Local Support
  • Compliance

Key Product Features

  • Windows-Based Application: Works seamlessly in both single and multi-user environments.
  • Payroll Management: Easily manage salaries, bonuses, deductions, taxes and more.
  • Employee Database: Keep employee records secure and easily accessible.
  • E-Payslips: Send digital payslips directly to employees.
  • Customizable Reporting: Generate reports tailored to your business needs.
    • Payroll Training Services
