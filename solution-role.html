Employer of Record Solution (EOR)

Expand Your Business with CorePay's Employer of Record Solution

CorePay’s EOR solution allows you to hire employees across Southern Africa, including Zimbabwe, without setting up a local entity. We handle all HR tasks like payroll, taxes, benefits, and compliance, ensuring full legal adherence while you manage day-to-day operations.

What is an Employer of Record (EOR)?

An EOR is a third-party provider that legally employs workers on behalf of your company. CorePay becomes the official employer, managing all legal and administrative aspects while your employees perform their roles within your organization. This solution offers a seamless, compliant, and cost-effective way to employ talent in Southern Africa.
